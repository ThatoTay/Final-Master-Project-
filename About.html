WHAT DEFINES US?


Tay Promos And Events is an experienced turnkey events planning and management company with infrastructure capacity. They assist clients with all their event planning and management, furniture hire, decor and logistical needs for any size event. A passionate family orientated team with all the knowledge and skills one would need to host a memorable event. The Tay Promos And Events team is comprehensive, allowing them to put together all the little pieces that make an event distinct and memorable.

The company was found by Thato Mukhavhuli together with Lebogang Mukhavhuli and Shallot Dzudule, young females with big visions and greater ideas and an ethos of excellence, innovation, setting. We are 100% black female-owned and managed. Thato founded the company in order to change the way South Africans host events, with an ethos of excellence, innovation, trend setting and the best value offering.


















MEET THE FOUNDERS

Tay Promos and Events was found by Thato Mukhavhuli in the year 2020. A young girl of 20 years old with big dreams of being one of the greatest entrepreneur. she always had a dream of being an event organizer from a very young of 16 years, she taught herself being creative and innovate with a vision of implementing new and unique strategies of hosting events.

Thato Partnered with her sister Lebogang Mukhavhuli, who has so much love and very passionate about this business. Lebogang is very dedicated and passionate, she is very good with coming up with different styles of ideas. Together with the rest of our team, we aim to always give out our best. We are a team that never disappoint.
